# Finding Schema

Every design finding promoted from raw evidence must include:

- Finding title.
- Evidence screenshots with relative paths.
- Source site and captured viewport.
- Pattern category.
- Reasoning.
- Pros.
- Cons.
- Applicability to RTIH.
- Implementation complexity: low, medium, high.
- Risk analysis.
- Comparison against current RTIH.
- Comparison against Y Combinator.
- Comparison against Techstars.
- Comparison against a modern SaaS benchmark.
- Comparison against a government or institutional benchmark.
- Fit conclusion: authentic, trustworthy, usable, globally competitive, and scalable.

Raw screenshot records live in `research/findings/screenshot-evidence.jsonl`. They are evidence, not recommendations.
